Ziyarat text recited when visiting the Bibiheybat Shrine!

Stand facing the Qibla at the head of the holy shrine, recite Takbir (Allahu Akbar) 34 times, Subhanallah 33 times, and Alhamdulillah 33 times to praise Allah, then recite the following:

Assalamu ala Adam, Safwatillah

السَّلامُ عَـلى آدَمَ صَـفْوَةِ اللهِ

Assalamu ala Nuh, Nabiyyillah

السَّلامُ عَلى نوُح نَبِىِّ اللهِ

Assalamu ala Ibrahim, Khalilillah

السَّلامُ عَلى اِبْراهيمَ خَليلِ اللهِ

Assalamu ala Musa, Kalimillah

السَّلامُ عَلى موُسى كَليمِ اللهِ

Assalamu ala Isa, Ruhillah

السَّلامُ عَلى عيِسىَ روُحِ اللهِ

Assalamu alayka ya Rasulallah

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكَ يا رسول اللهِ

Assalamu alayka ya Khayra khalqillah

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكَ يا خَيْرَ خَلْقَ اللهِ

Assalamu alayka ya Safiyyallah

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكَ يا صَفِىَّ اللهِ

Assalamu alayka ya Muhammad ibn Abdillahi, Khataman-nabiyyin

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكَ يا مُحَمّدَ بْنَ عَبْدِ اللهِ خاتَمَ النَّبِيّينَ

Assalamu alayka ya Amir al-Mu'minin, Aliyyibna Abi Talib, wasiyya Rasulillah

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكَ يا اَميرَ الْمُؤْمِنينَ عَلىَّ بْنَ اَبى طالِب وَصِىَّ رَسوُلِ اللهِ

Assalamu alayki ya Fatima, Sayyidatan-nisa il-alamin

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ يا فاطِمَةُ سَيِّدَةَ نِساءِ الْعالَمينَ

Assalamu alaykuma ya Sibtay nabiyyir-rahmati wa Sayyiday shababi ahlil-jannah

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكُما يا سِبْطَىْ نَبِىِّ الرَّحْمَةِ وَ سَيِّدَىْ شَبابِ أَهْلِ الْجَنَّةِ

Assalamu alayka ya Aliyyibnal-Husayn, Sayyidal-abidin wa Qurrata aynin-nazirin

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكَ يا عَلِىَّ بْنَ الْحُسَيْنِ سَيِّدَ الْعابِدينَ وَ قُرَّةَ عَيْنِ النّاظِرِينَ

Assalamu alayka ya Muhammadibna Ali, Baqiral-ilmi ba'dan-Nabiyy

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكَ يا مُحَمَّدَ بْنَ عَلِىٍّ باقِرَ الْعِلْمِ بَعْدَ النَّبِىِّ

Assalamu alayka ya Ja'faribna Muhammad, as-Sadiq al-Barr al-Amin

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكَ يا جَعْفَرَ بْنَ مُحَمَّد الصّادِقَ الْبارَّ الْامينَ

Assalamu alayka ya Musabna Ja'far, at-Tahir at-Tuhr

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكَ يا موُسَى بْنَ جَعْفَر الّطّاهِرَ الطُّهْرَ

Assalamu alayka ya Aliyyibna Musa, ar-Rida al-Murtada

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكَ يا عَلِىِّ بْنَ موُسَى الرِّضَا الْمُرْتَضى

Assalamu alayka ya Muhammadibna Ali, at-Taqiyy

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكَ يا مُحَمَّدَ بْنَ عَلِى التَّقِىَّ

Assalamu alayka ya Aliyyibna Muhammad, an-Naqiyy an-Nasih al-Amin

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكَ يا عَلِىِّ بْنَ مُحَمَّد النَّقِىَّ النّاصِحَ الْأَمينَ

Assalamu alayka ya Hasanibna Aliyy

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكَ يا حَسَنَ بْنَ عَلِىٍّ

Assalamu alal-Wasiyyi min ba'dih

السَّلامُ عَلَى الْوَصِىِّ مِنْ بَعْدِه

Allahumma salli ala nurika wa sirajika wa waliyyi waliyyika wa wasiyyi wasiyyika wa hujjatika ala khalqika

اَللّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلى نُورِكَ وَ سِراجِكَ وَ وَلِىِّ وَلِيِّكَ وَ وَصِىِّ وَصِيِّكَ وَ حُجَّتِكَ عَلى خَلْقِكَ

Assalamu alayki ya binta Rasulillah

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ يا بِنْتَ رَسوُلِ اللّه

Assalamu alayki ya binta Fatimata wa Khadijah

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ يا بِنْتَ فاطِمَةَ وَ خَديجَةَ

Assalamu alayki ya binta Amiril-mu'minin

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ يا بِنْتَ اَميرِ الْمُؤْمِنينَ

Assalamu alayki ya bintal-Hasani wal-Husayn

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ يا بِنْتَ الْحَسَنِ وَ الْحُسَيْنِ

Assalamu alayki ya binta Waliyyillah

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ يا بِنْتَ وَلِىِّ اللهِ

Assalamu alayki ya ukhta Waliyyillah

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ يا اُخْتَ وَلِىِّ اللهِ

Assalamu alayki ya ammata Waliyyillah

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ يا عَمَّةَ وَلِىِّ اللهِ

Assalamu alayki ya binta Musabni Ja'far, wa rahmatullahi wa barakatuh

السَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ يا بِنْتَ موُسَى بْنِ جَعْفَر وَ رَحْمَةُ اللهِ وَ بَرَكاتُهُ

Assalamu alayki arrafallahu baynana wa baynakum fil-jannati wa hasharana fi zumratikum wa awradana hawda Nabiyyukum wa saqana bika'si jaddikum min yadi Aliyyibni Abi Talib, salawatullahi alaykum

السَّلامُ عَلَيْك ِعَرَّفَ اللّهُ بَيْنَنا وَ بَيْنَكُمْ فِى الْجَنَّة ِوَ حَشَرَنا فى زُمْرَتِكُمْ وَ أَوَرَدْنا حَوْضَ نَبِيِّكُمْ وَ سَقانا بِكَأْسِ جَدِّكُمْ مِنْ يَد عَلِى ِّ بْنِ اَبى طالِب صَلَواتُ اللّه عَلَيْكُمْ

As'alullaha an yuriyena fikumus-surura wal-faraj, wa an yajma'ana wa iyyakum fi zumrati jaddikum Muhammad, sallallahu alayhi wa alih, wa an la yaslubana ma'rifatakum, innahu Waliyyun Qadir

أَسْئَلُ اللّه أَنْ يُرِيَنا فيكُمُ السُّروُرَ وَ الْفَرَجَ وَ أَنْ يَجْمَعَنا وَ إِيّاكُمْ فى زُمْرَةِ جَدِّكُمْ مُحَمَّد صَلَّى اللّهُ عَلَيْهِ وَ آلِه ِوَ أَنْ لا يَسْلُبَنا مَعْرفَتَكُمْ إِنَّهُ وَلِىِّ قَديرٌ

Ataqarrabu ilallahi bihubbikum wal-bara'ati min a'da'ikum, wattaslimi ilallahi raziyyan bihi ghayra munkirin wa la mustakbirin, wa ala yaqini ma ata bihi Muhammadun wa bihi razin, natlubu bizalika wajhaka ya Sayyidi!

أَتَقَرَّبُ إِلَى اللهِ بِحُبِّكُمْ وَ الْبَرإَةِ مِنْ أَعْدائِكُمْ وَ التَّسْلِيمِ إِلَى اللّه رَاضِياً بِهِ غَيْرَ مُنْكِر وَ لا مُسْتَكْبِرٍ وَ عَلى يَقينِ ما أَتى بِهِ مَحَمَّدٌ وَ بِهَ راض نَطْلُبُ بِذلِكَ وَجْهِكَ يا سَيِّدى

Allahumma wa ridaka wad-daral-akhirah

اَللّهُمَّ وَ رِضاكَ وَ الدّارَ الْآخِرَةِ

Ya Fatimatushfa'i li fil-jannati, fa'inna laki indallahi sha'nan minash-sha'n

يا فاطِمَةُ اِشْفَعى لى فِى الْجَنَّةِ فَاِنَّ لَكِ عِنْدَاللهِ شَأْناً مِنَ الشَّأْنِ

Allahumma inni as'aluka an takhtima li bis-sa'adati, fala taslub minni ma ana fihi wa la hawla wa la quwwata illa billahil-Aliyyil-Azim

اَللّْهُمّ اِنّى اَسْئَلُكَ أَنْ تَخْتِمَ لى بِالسَّعادَةِ فَلا تَسْلُبْ مِنّىِ ما أَنَا فيهِ وَ لا حُولَ وَ لا قُوَّةَ إِلاّ بالّله الْعَلِىِّ الْعَظيمِ

Allahummastajib lana wa taqabbalhu bikaramika wa izzatika wa birahmatika wa afiyatik

اَللّهُمَ اسْتَجِبْ لَنا وَ تَقَبَّلْهُ بِكَرَمِكَ وَ عِزَّتِكَ وَ بِرَحْمَتِكَ وَ عافِيَتَكَ

Wa sallallahu ala Muhammadin wa alihi ajma'ina wa sallama taslima

وَ صَلَّى اللهُ عَلى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ أَجْمَعينَ وَ سَلَّمَ تَسْليما

Ya Arhamar-Rahimin

يا أَرْحَمَ الرّاحِمينَ

ENGLISH TRANSLATION OF THE ZIYARAT TEXT

Peace be upon Adam, the Chosen of Allah!

Peace be upon Nuh (Noah), the Prophet of Allah!

Peace be upon Ibrahim (Abraham), the Friend of Allah!

Peace be upon Musa (Moses), the One who spoke with Allah!

Peace be upon Isa (Jesus), the Spirit of Allah!

Peace be upon you, O Messenger of Allah!

Peace be upon you, O best of Allah's creation!

Peace be upon you, O Chosen one of Allah!

Peace be upon you, O Muhammad ibn Abdullah, the Seal of the Prophets!

Peace be upon you, O Commander of the Faithful, Ali ibn Abi Talib, the successor of the Messenger of Allah!

Peace be upon you, O Fatimah, the Leader of all the women of the worlds!

Peace be upon you both, O two grandsons of the Prophet of Mercy and the two leaders of the youth of Paradise!

Peace be upon you, O Ali ibn al-Husayn, the leader of the worshippers and the delight of the eyes of the onlookers!

Peace be upon you, O Muhammad ibn Ali, the one who split open knowledge after the Prophet!

Peace be upon you, O Ja'far ibn Muhammad, the truthful, the righteous, the trustworthy!

Peace be upon you, O Musa ibn Ja'far, the pure, the purified!

Peace be upon you, O Ali ibn Musa, the one pleased with by God and pleasing to God!

Peace be upon you, O Muhammad ibn Ali, the pious!

Peace be upon you, O Ali ibn Muhammad, the pure, the advisor, the trustworthy!

Peace be upon you, O Hasan ibn Ali!

Peace be upon the successor after him!

O Allah, send Your blessings upon Your light and Your lamp, the guardian of Your guardian, the successor of Your successor, and Your proof upon Your creation!

Peace be upon you, O daughter of the Messenger of Allah!

Peace be upon you, O daughter of Fatimah and Khadijah!

Peace be upon you, O daughter of the Commander of the Faithful!

Peace be upon you, O daughter of Hasan and Husayn!

Peace be upon you, O daughter of the Guardian of Allah!

Peace be upon you, O sister of the Guardian of Allah!

Peace be upon you, O aunt of the Guardian of Allah!

Peace be upon you, O daughter of Musa ibn Ja'far! May the mercy of Allah and His blessings be upon you!

Peace be upon you. May Allah introduce us to you in Paradise, gather us in your company, bring us to the Pool of your Prophet, and give us drink from the cups of your grandfather by the hand of Ali ibn Abi Talib. May the blessings of Allah be upon you all.

I ask Allah to show us in you joy and relief, to gather us and you in the company of your grandfather Muhammad (peace be upon him and his family), and not to deprive us of your recognition. Indeed, He is our Guardian and All-Powerful.

O my Master, my Lord, with all of this I seek Your countenance, Your pleasure, and the Hereafter.

O Fatimah, intercede for me in Paradise, for you have a great station with Allah!

O Allah, I ask You to seal my life with happiness and not to take away what I have. There is no power or strength except with Allah, the Most High, the Most Great.

O Allah, answer our prayers and accept them by Your generosity, Your might, Your mercy, and Your well-being.

And may Allah send blessings upon Muhammad and all his family, and peace be upon them.

O Most Merciful of the Merciful!

Bihar al-Anwar, vol. 99, p. 266
